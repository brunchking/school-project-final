Mountain Ali

❀2019 Alishan Cherry Blossom Season
❀Ali Mountain Flowering Period: March to April of each year
❀2019 Alishan cherry blossom season flowering forecast: 3/9~4/15
❀Cherry blossom location: Alishan.

Transportation:
❀Drive by yourself:
-National Highway No. 3 - under the Zhongpu Interchange - Provincial Highway 18.
❀Public transportation:：
-Take the Taitung Railway to Chiayi Station - take the Chiayi Bus (to Alishan) and get off at Alishan Station.
-Take the Taitung Railway to Chiayi Station - transfer to the Alishan Forest Railway at Alishan Station.
